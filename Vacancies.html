Our Vacancies

Nurse - Job Requirements

  • Valid nursing license and certifications.
  • Minimum 2 years of clinical experience.
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills.
  • Ability to handle emergency situations efficiently.
  • Compassionate and patient-focused care approach.

Medical Officer - Job Requirements

  • MBBS degree with a valid medical license.
  • Minimum 3 years of experience in a hospital or clinical setting.
  • Strong diagnostic and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent communication and leadership abilities.
  • Ability to handle emergencies and critical cases effectively.
  • Commitment to patient-centered care and ethical practices.

At Cambridge Hospital, we value our medical officers for their dedication and expertise. Join our team to work in a state-of-the-art facility with access to the latest medical technologies and a supportive work environment.
